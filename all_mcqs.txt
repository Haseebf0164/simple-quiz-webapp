1. What is the derivative of x^2?
A) x
B) 2x
C) x^2
D) 2
Answer: B

2. What is the integral of 1/x dx?
A) x
B) ln|x|
C) 1/x
D) e^x
Answer: B

3. What is the limit of sin(x)/x as x approaches 0?
A) 0
B) 1
C) Infinity
D) -1
Answer: B

4. What is the derivative of sin(x)?
A) cos(x)
B) -cos(x)
C) -sin(x)
D) tan(x)
Answer: A

5. What is the area under the curve y = x from x = 0 to x = 1?
A) 0.5
B) 1
C) 2
D) 0
Answer: A
